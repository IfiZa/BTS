The group...

BTS (Korean: Î∞©ÌÉÑÏÜåÎÖÑÎã®; romanization: Bangtan Sonyeondan, lit. Bulletproof Boy Scouts - but also called Beyond The Scene), is a South Korean boy band consisting of 7 members, namely: RM, Jin, SUGA, j-hope, Jimin, V and Jung Kook.

Their fandom...

BTS is supported, since its beginning, by a hugely loyal, dedicated, proud and active community, known as A R M Y (Adorable Representative M.C. for Youth).

Their accolades...

Almost 10 years since their debut, the group has uncountable accolades from repeated number-one songs in charts around the world, concequent sold-out concerts, numerous awards, Grammy nominations, S Korea's and major brands' ambassadoring to addressing, thrice, the United Nations General Assembly!

Their state of play...

They are currently on a pause from their group activities and focusing on their individual projects, while Jin has already enlisted for the mandatory South Korean military service, and j-hope will be the next one to follow. Their much waited reunion is foreseen for 2025, and, we, ARMY, can't wait! And "...the best is YET TO COME"!

Their debut...

They debuted on June 13, 2013 under Big Hit Music (currently part of HYBE Labels) with the lead single 'No More Dream' from the album '2 Cool 4 Skool'.

Their music and lyrics...

Their musical style incorporates a wide range of genres like pop, hip-hop, rap, R&B, dance/electronic, latin pop, urban neosoul and many more, and their lyrics focus on social issues, inclusivity, mental health, self-love and the struggles of youth, among others. They co-produce and co-write most of their songs.
