Outline

Their musical style incorporates a wide range of genres (pop, hip-hop, R&B, dance/electronic etc), while their lyrics focus on social issues, mental health and the youth. The co-produce and co-write most of their songs. By 2022 the group has uncountable accolades from repeated number-one songs in charts around the world, to concequent sold-out concerts, numerous awards and brands ambassadoring to addressing, thrice, the United Nations General Assembly and, most importantly, having the most loyal, dedicated, proud and enormous fanbase, known as A R M Y (Adorable Representative M.C. for Youth.)

They are currently on pause of their group activities and focusing on their individual projects, while Jin has already enlisted for the mandatory South Korean military service, and J-Hope will be the next one to follow shortly.

Their much waited reunion is foreseen for 2025, and, we, ARMYs, can't wait, right?!!!!!!
