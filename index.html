Their name...

BTS (Korean: Î∞©ÌÉÑÏÜåÎÖÑÎã®; Romanization: Bangtan Sonyeondan; lit. Bulletproof Boy Scouts), is a South Korean boy band consisting of 7 members, namely: RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V and Jungkook (in the order they joined the group).

Their debut...

They debuted on June 13, 2013 under Big Hit Music (currently part of HYBE Labels) with the lead single 'No More Dream' from the album '2 Cool 4 Skool'.

Their music and lyrics...

Their musical style incorporates a wide range of genres pop, hip-hop, R&B, dance/electronic, latin pop, urban neosoul and many more, and their lyrics focus on social issues, mental health and the youth. They co-produce and co-write most of their songs.

Their fanbase...

BTS is supported since its beginning by a hugely loyal, dedicated, proud and active community, known as A R M Y (Adorable Representative M.C. for Youth.)
"ARMY stands apart from other fandoms through the ways it has mobilsed with an unrivaled level of organization..."
- Time magazine, Nov 2020

Their accolades...

Almost 10 years since their debut, the group has uncountable accolades from repeated number-one songs in charts around the world, to concequent sold-out concerts, numerous awards and brands ambassadoring to addressing, thrice, the United Nations General Assembly.

In 2023...

They are currently on pause of their group activities and focusing on their individual projects, while Jin has already enlisted for the mandatory South Korean military service, and J-Hope will be the next one to follow shortly. Their much waited reunion is foreseen for 2025, and, we, ARMY, can't wait!!!
